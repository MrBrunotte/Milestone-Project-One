Coaching Program

what is it?

Your three months of comprehensive vegan coaching support begins with a one-hour initial consultation to gather background information and establish your personal goals. We will continue working together over the next three months, with six 45-minute follow-up sessions, plus two 60-minute sessions (or any combination that fits your schedule), during which we will develop effective transitional strategies to help you seriously get on trac, and then really stay on track with your new healthy lifestyle. This 90-day program includes personalized recipes and customized menu plans, plus unlimited email support. In the Nassau, Bahamas area, your Comprehensive Coaching Program can also include a personal pantry makeover or grocery shopping tour, as well as scheuled cooking sessions together.
